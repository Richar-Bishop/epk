About Richard Bishop

Richard Bishop is an independent hard rock / blues-rock artist and U.S. military veteran from Fairfield, Iowa. His music channels the invisible wounds of service, generational values, loss, resilience, and the everyday fight to keep standing.

No label. No backing. Eleven tracks built from scratch by a single father who refused to quit. Drop-C, baritone, no apologies.
