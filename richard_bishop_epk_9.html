About Richard Bishop

Richard Bishop is an independent hard rock / blues-rock artist and U.S. military veteran from Fairfield, Iowa. His music is raw, honest, and deeply personal — channeling the invisible wounds of service, generational values, loss, resilience, and the everyday fight to keep standing.

No label. No backing. Just one man, a baritone guitar in Drop-C, and a lifetime of blood, dirt, and hard-won stories.
